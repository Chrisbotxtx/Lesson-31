Codingal is a pioneering online platform dedicated to teaching coding to kids and teens aged 6 to 18. With a mission toinspire young learners to embrace programming, Codingal offers live, interactive classes led by expert instructors whoare rigorously vetted and trained in computer science. The platform provides a personalized learning experience, allowing students to learn at their own pace through one-on-one sessions or small group classes. At Codingal, students engage in project-based learning, creating apps, games, animations, and websites, which not only makes the process fun but also fosters creativity and critical thinking. The curriculum covers various programming languages, including Python, Java, and Scratch, ensuring that learners acquire essential skills relevant in today’s digital landscape. Codingal's approach is designed to empower children with the knowledge and confidence they need to become future innovators—whether as entrepreneurs, engineers, or scientists. Since its launch in 2020, Codingal has successfully reached over 500,000 students worldwide, emphasizing the importance of coding education at an early age. By combining gamification techniques with a supportive learning environment, Codingal aims to make coding an enjoyable and enriching experience for every child.
